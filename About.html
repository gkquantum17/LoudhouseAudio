About Loudhouse

For far too long, music artists have had no platform to capture their spontaneous, musical ideas -- brilliant ideas that could be forgotten they don't become tangible immediately. For far too long, musicians have not had the opportunity to seamlessly collaborate with others from around the world to create original music together. The Loudhouse audio development platform addresses these issues.

Loudhouse is a fast, beautiful, and fun way to share your musical life with friends and other musicians. Using the Loudhouse mobile application, record your musical ideas on the go and upload it immediately to your Loudhouse account. Later, you can listen to all your stockpiled musical ideas and use them as inspiration for a part of your next song! With our built-in, professional audio-editing software, we will ensure you will have all the tools necessary to produce incredible music. Our core innovation is that we enable you to share your brilliant, lyrical creations with other users who can offer feedback, edit, and even contribute to your ideas with pieces of their own!
