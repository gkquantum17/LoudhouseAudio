The Loudhouse Platform

Loudhouse is a social networking platform which enables recreational and professional music artists from around the world to capture and share their spontaneous, musical ideas, which they can then turn into original music, together.

Step One

Record your spontaneous musical ideas using any mobile device.

Step Two

Upload your recording to your Loudhouse online profile with the click of a button.

Step Three

Edit your recording using Loudhouse's built-in, audio-editing software.

Step Four

Connect with other Loudhouse users using the built-in social networking feature.

Step Five

Share your piece with your Loudhouse network so they can add on to your unfinished song.

Step Six

Repeat until you finish your awesome song!

Step One: Record

Brilliant musical ideas come unpredictably - whether you're with out with your friends, in school, at work, or on the road. Be prepared to record those unique, spontaneous musical chimes, rhymes, and lyrics on the go with the Loudhouse mobile app. This recording will become one of many musical ideas that you will have stockpiled and have ready to pick from as you get ready to make your next song. (The mobile application is in development and will become available soon for the Apple, Android, and Windows platforms.)

Step Two: Upload

With the click of a button, you can upload your recorded musical piece to your Loudhouse account. You will have access to all of your recorded music on your account for you to choose from.

Step Three: Edit

Here's the fun part! With your Loudhouse account, you can use our built-in, professional audio-editing software to edit or recreate one of your stockpiled, recorded audio pieces - or even start fresh with a completely new musical piece! Our editing software is incredibly user-friendly, easy to learn, and has all the tools necessary to make your song stand out among your competitors. Save your musical piece - whether a full song or just a verse or chorus - so you can easily edit or add to it later!

Step Four: Connect

As a social networking site, we enable you to search for and connect with other Loudhouse users from around the world. Add them to your Loudhouse Network so that you can work with them on future songs, ask for feedback, be a part of an amazing group of musicians like you, and even do a few more awesome things that we will show you in the next step!

Step Five: Share

With your Loudhouse Network that we told you about in the previous step, you can take advantage of our core innovation. You can share your recorded or edited musical pieces, even as an uncompleted song, with your network. But the Loudhouse platform doesn't just let users merely listen to the music that others created, we empower our listeners to become the creators. Loudhouse enables other users to edit and even contribute to your musical ideas, and vice versa -- allowing for users from around the world to collaborate to create original music, together.

Step Six: Repeat

Repeat these steps until you are satisfied with your song! What if your song becomes a hit? Check out the royalties contract here!
